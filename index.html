Who We Are

Decades of Industrial Insight

Our History

LingKong was founded in 2010 by a group of industrial engineers and supply chain experts who recognized a critical gap in the HVLS market. For years, factory owners struggled to find reliable data amidst a sea of marketing noise. We started as a small consultancy offering bespoke ventilation audits, but quickly realized the industry needed a standardized, transparent ranking system. Over the last decade, we have grown into China's premier authority on industrial ventilation, compiling data from thousands of installations across the nation.

Our Mission

Our core mission is simple: to empower industrial enterprises with the clarity they need to optimize their environments. We believe that efficiency, safety, and employee comfort are paramount in modern manufacturing. By rigorously testing and ranking HVLS fan manufacturers, we compel the industry to raise its standards while helping our clients avoid costly procurement mistakes. We value integrity, data transparency, and engineering excellence above all else, ensuring every recommendation we make stands the test of time.
